Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this personal website is to serve as a comprehensive landing page for prospective employers, showcasing the creator's front-end development skills and providing a platform for sharing and storing personal projects. With a clean and modern design, the website will serve as a digital resume accessible through links on various online profiles, such as LinkedIn, and through QR codes on physical resumes. The website will also provide an introduction to the creator and their programming goals, allowing potential employers to better understand their background and interests even before the interview process.
